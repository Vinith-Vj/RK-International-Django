Project Planing
Involves outlining the steps and resources needed for a successful project. It includes defining objectives, setting timelines, budgeting, and allocating responsibilities. Effective planning ensures coordination among teams, mitigates risks, and provides a clear roadmap, ensuring the project stays on track and meets both time and quality standards.
Architecture
Focuses on designing functional and aesthetically pleasing structures. It involves planning layouts, ensuring safety regulations, and considering environmental factors. Architects work closely with clients to create innovative designs that reflect the desired vision while balancing form, function, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness, ensuring a seamless transition from concept to reality.
Construction
Involves the physical execution of architectural plans, from site preparation to building completion. This phase includes earthwork, foundation laying, structural work, and installation of utilities. It requires skilled labor, proper equipment, and adherence to safety standards. Timely, high-quality construction ensures that the project meets specifications and is ready for use.

Years of Experience

Providing the best quality service

RK International started as a small scale civil works contractor in the Sultanate of Oman in the year 1990 under the name of Salim and Abdulla ibn Muhammad Nasser Al Saadi Trading and Contracting.

During the years of the boom, the company developed from being a sub-contractor for industry giants at the time Qurum Contracting, Galfar, Ajay Enterprises, Century Contracting, Muscat Engineering and Contracting LLC etc, to being a well- established main contractor thanks to the jobs that were conducted for esteemed clients such as the Ministry of Education, Ministry of Defence, Shell Oman Marketing Company SAOG, Al Maha Petroleum Marketing Company SAOG, Sultan Qaboos University and various other government as well as private firms for jobs valued at 3 million Omani Riyals or more.

Currently, the company specializes in projects relating to Roadworks for esteemed clients as well as commercial buildings & petrol stations all around the country.

Roadworks

Encompass a variety of essential activities, including surface and shoulder preparation, earthwork, asphalt works. These processes ensure proper alignment, structural stability, and durability. Additionally, roadworks focus on enhancing safety, improving traffic flow, and minimizing environmental impact to create sustainable and reliable transportation infrastructure.

Filling station

Involves a comprehensive range of activities, including civil works for site preparation, pipeline works for fuel distribution, mechanical installations, electrical systems for operations and safety, and structural works to ensure durability. These tasks collectively create efficient, safe, and reliable fuel dispensing facilities.

Construction

Encompass a diverse range of structures, including schools, university buildings, and shopping complexes. These projects require careful planning, design, and construction to meet functional, aesthetic, and safety standards, ensuring spaces are efficient, sustainable, and tailored to serve educational, commercial, and community needs effectively.

Heavy vehicles and machinery

Alongside crew services, include specialized teams such as asphalt crews and earthwork crews. These skilled workers, equipped with appropriate machinery, play a crucial role in executing tasks efficiently, ensuring the timely completion of construction, roadwork, and other heavy-duty projects.
